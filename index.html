About Us

Isang organisasyon ng mga Kabataang Intersadong Nagsasanay sa Tela, Aspili at Bulaklak.

KINTAB Catering is a dynamic organization formed by young individuals passionate about developing their skills in fabric, thread, and floral arrangements. What began as a training initiative for students has grown into a professional catering service, committed to transforming every event into a memorable experience.
